About Placements at MSRIT

The Department of Placement and Training was established in 1997. MSRIT has excellent placement records. About 90% of students who seek campus placements are placed successfully. The training and placement cell coordinates with leading organizations that recruit engineers and graduates for entry-level positions.

Placement Highlights

Highest Package: 50 LPA
Average Package: 7.66 LPA
Companies Visited: 358
Total Job Offers: 1892

The biggest names in the corporate world visit the campus on a regular basis. MSRIT is one of the preferred academic institutes for a large number of organizations for recruiting graduates. Companies from IT, core engineering, management, and other sectors recruit from MSRIT.

Training Provided

Aptitude tests, group discussions, mock interviews are conducted regularly to prepare students. Soft skills training programs are also arranged. Students get opportunities to work on live projects with government and non-government firms.

Contact: Department of Placement and Training, First floor, Administrative block, MSRIT, Bangalore - 560054.

Hostel at MSRIT

MSRIT provides hostel accommodation for both boys and girls separately. The hostels can accommodate up to 1500 students total.

For exact hostel fee amounts and current charges, please contact:
MSRIT Hostel Office directly or visit msrit.edu for the latest fee structure.

Hostel Facilities Included

Well-equipped rooms for Single and Double occupancy with attached or common bathrooms with hot water supply
Furniture provided: Cot, Chair, Almirah, Reading table
Two spacious dining halls
Vegetarian mess with Breakfast, Lunch, Evening Snacks and Special Dinner (North and South Indian menu)
Free Laundry facility
Spacious recreation halls with LED TV, newspaper and magazines
Indoor and outdoor games facility
Night canteen up to 11 PM
Health center with senior doctor (Monday to Saturday 5 PM to 7 PM), free medicines
Round the clock security
Free Wi-Fi
CCTV surveillance

Girls Hostel

MSRIT has a separate hostel for girls with similar facilities.

Fee Structure at MSRIT

Important Note

MSRIT fee structure varies by course, category (Government/Management/NRI quota), and academic year. For exact and updated fee amounts, please contact the admissions office or visit msrit.edu/admissions.

Contact for Fee Details

Admissions Office, MSRIT, MSR Nagar, MSRIT Post, Bangalore - 560054
Phone: 080-23600822, 23608445
Website: www.msrit.edu

Admissions are open for UG courses under Management, NRI, PIO and Foreign National quota.

Eligibility for B.E. Admission

Candidates who have passed 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with Chemistry, Biotechnology, Biology, Computer Science or Electronics. Selection is based on CET/COMED-K scores for government quota and management quota separately.
